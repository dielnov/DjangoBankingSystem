Our Products & Services

Motor Vehicle Loans

A Vehicle loan is an automobile financing to enable the customer to purchase brand new or second hand vehicles.NOTE: WE DO NOT FINANCE GREY IMPORT SECOND HAND VEHICLES, ( e.g. Japan and Singapore).

Personal Loans

A personal loan is an advance extended to customers who maintain current accounts with BancABC and has proof of consistent income. It is based on the borrower’s credit rating and has a variable interest rate over a fixed term. The capital as well as interest is repaid monthly from the customer’s current / savings account over the agreed period.

Civil Servant Loans

Special Civil Servant loans are accessible from the loan portal. Create your loan portal, and apply for a Civil Servant Loan by submiting your payslip and other required documents. Once the documents are processed your loan will be granted and you will be notified through your portal.

Mortgage

A loan offered to customers to purchase for residential purpose.The Loan term is selected by the customer (minimum repayment term being 7 years and maximum being 20 years). The monthly installment is deducted directly from the current account or by direct debit from another bank.

The Loan Portal?

The Loan Portal is a BancABC platform to allow clients around the nations to apply for loans without hustle. It removes the need to unnecessarily move around to and fro BancABC offices just to apply for a loan and submit documents. With the Loan Portal clients can simply apply for their desired loans in the comfort of their homes, work place or on the go anywher ein the world. Loan Application Has never Been this Easy!

Verified Dealers

For Motor Vehicle and Housing Loans, the Loan Portal provides acccess to vehicles and houses on sale from truested dealers verified by the bank. Their products are evaluated by the bank and as such, you can easily purchase them with your loan on the site.

Loan Tracker

Keep track of your loan application progress from the moment of application. With the Loan portal, you can also keep track of your loan balance.

Live Rates

The Loan Portal keeps a live update of the prevailing interest rates as well as exchange rates.
